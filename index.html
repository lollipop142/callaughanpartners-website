Welcome to Callaughan Partners

Your partners in financial success

Callaughan Partners is a full-service accounting and advisory firm dedicated to helping individuals, businesses, and investors make confident financial decisions. We combine deep technical expertise with a genuinely personalised approach.

From tax compliance and bookkeeping to sophisticated asset protection strategies and self-managed superfunds, our team provides comprehensive support at every stage of your financial journey.

Our Services

From everyday tax obligations to complex investment structures, Callaughan Partners offers the full spectrum of accounting, advisory, and financial services your business needs to thrive.

Comprehensive tax planning, preparation, and lodgement for individuals and businesses. We ensure full compliance while identifying every legitimate opportunity to minimise your tax burden — year after year.

Strategic guidance to grow and protect your business. From financial analysis and cash flow planning to business structuring and succession — we help you make decisions with confidence.

Specialist advice and administration for SMSFs. We help trustees navigate compliance requirements, annual reporting, and strategic planning so your super works harder for your future.

From entity structuring to negative gearing and capital gains planning, we support property investors with tailored advice that protects returns and strengthens long-term wealth creation.

Protecting what you have built matters. We advise on trusts, companies, and effective ownership structures that help safeguard your personal and business assets against unnecessary exposure.

Proactive tax planning is at the heart of smart financial management. We work with you throughout the year to reduce surprises, improve cash flow, and make strategic tax decisions before year end.

Accurate, up-to-date bookkeeping keeps your business running smoothly. We provide reliable support across reconciliations, reporting, payroll, and systems so you can focus on growth.

Need to fund equipment, vehicles, or business expansion? We help assess finance options with a clear understanding of tax implications, repayment impact, and long-term business outcomes.

Working alongside trusted buyers agents, we provide the financial analysis and due diligence you need to invest with clarity — ensuring every acquisition decision is built on solid numbers.
